Boutique Refits
Sometimes an interior does not need to be entirely changed, or the boat schedule does not allow more than a short break on shore. This is when you might consider a minimal "boutique" refit to freshen up an interior.

The projects shown here, reflect how an interior can be transformed, just by adding some accents: new furnishings, fabrics and art selected or commission just for your yacht or home.
